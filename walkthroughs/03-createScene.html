03 — Building the World

Creates the Scene container, two lights, and the ground plane.

What this file does

Creates a Scene — the container that holds every light, mesh, and camera in your game world. It needs the Engine to know how to render itself.

Adds a HemisphericLight — soft ambient light from directly above, like an overcast sky. Gives everything a base brightness (intensity 0.6).

Adds a DirectionalLight (the sun) — comes from a fixed angle, creating directional shading and the feel of sunlight (intensity 0.8).

Creates a flat ground mesh (40×40 units) with a green material, then returns the completed scene back to Game.ts.

What Gets Built — Animated

DirectionalLight — "sun"
Shines from upper-left-front. Creates directional shading that makes 3D shapes feel solid.
direction: Vector3(-1,-2,-1) · intensity: 0.8

MeshBuilder.CreateGround — "ground"
A flat plane at Y=0. Green material applied using RGB values (each channel is 0.0–1.0).
width: 40 · height: 40 · Color3(0.3, 0.6, 0.2) ≈ forest green
